Privacy Policy

An original adventure by St3althWarri0r

Effective: July 10, 2026
The short version: Princess Polina collects no data. Not a little — none. Nothing you do in the game ever leaves your device. The rest of this page is just that sentence, in detail.

What this covers

This policy covers the Princess Polina mobile game (subtitle: and the Dragon Ivan) for Android and iOS, published by St3althWarri0r.

What we collect

Nothing. The game has no accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, no crash reporting, no ads, no tracking, and no third-party software development kits. It never asks who you are, and it has no way to find out.

What we share

Nothing — there is nothing to share. No data is sent to us or to anyone else.

Internet access

The game is fully playable offline and never connects to the internet. On Android it requests no permissions at all — not even internet access — so it could not send data anywhere even if it wanted to. On iOS it makes no network connections.

Your game saves

Your progress — save slots, settings, found secrets — is stored only in the game's local storage on your own device. It is never uploaded or backed up by us, and we cannot see or restore it. Uninstalling the game deletes it.

Children's privacy

The game is family-friendly, and this policy is too: we collect no personal information from anyone — including children. There is nothing to consent to, nothing to review, and nothing for a parent to request deletion of, because nothing is ever collected (this is how we comply with COPPA and similar laws).

Changes to this policy

If a future update ever changes any of this — for example, an optional update that adds ads — we will update this page and the app-store listings before that version is released, and change the effective date at the top. As long as the date above reads July 10, 2026, everything on this page is exactly as it was at launch.
